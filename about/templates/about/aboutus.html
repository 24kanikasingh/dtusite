OUR MISSION

This website focuses on feedback and views related to faculty members and department professors. User-generated content makes Assess Dtu Professor the highest trafficked site for quickly researching and rating professors across the university.

Now, students can comment on and rate their professors as well, by visiting professor's profile.

Assess DTU Professor is built for college students, by college students.Giving a genuine feedback for a professor is a rite of passage for every student, and connecting with peers on the site has become a key way for millions of students to navigate this process.To avoid offensive use of languages, students holding an account on this site are only allowed to rate their professor.
